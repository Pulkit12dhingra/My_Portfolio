Internship Experience

Internships are one of the best mediums to learn. I had done several internships to boost my strength and get my knowledge at par with the industry-level implementation. I had listed all my internships below:-

  • Data Scientist Intern at Ardberry Tech. Prvt. Ltd.

    The internship was at the Research and Development department of Ardberry Technologies Private Limited. The company is architectural-based and I worked on a project related to security and penetration. The project was titled 'Sign to Unlock' and it was about hand detection and tracking to make a pattern affiliated to the user who is registered. It had modern-day technologies like face detection included but it was more likely a cool way of doing the regular thing. The internship provided me a gateway to understanding new things and how an actual project is developed in the industry.

    Geeks For Geeks is a renowned computer science portal, consisting of a lot of stuff from articles to programming questions, interview experiences, and more. In the internship, I contribute multiple articles on Python and R programming languages. I had also contributed articles on Machine Learning and Apache Spark's pyspark. Volunteership Experience
